Masala-Gobhi




Here's a recipe for Masala Gobhi (Spiced Cauliflower):
Ingredients:
1 medium head of cauliflower, cut into florets
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
1 green chili, finely chopped (adjust to your spice preference)
1 medium tomato, finely chopped
½ teaspoon turmeric powder
1 teaspoon red chili powder
1 ½ teaspoons coriander powder
½ teaspoon cumin powder
½ teaspoon garam masala
Salt to taste
2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:
Prepare the Cauliflower: Wash the cauliflower florets thoroughly. You can blanch them in hot water for 2-3 minutes if you prefer a slightly softer texture. Drain well.

Sauté Aromatics: Heat the vegetable oil in a pan or skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.

Add Ginger-Garlic and Chili: Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and chopped green chili. Sauté for another minute until the raw smell disappears.
Cook Tomatoes: Add the finely chopped tomato to the pan. Cook until the tomatoes soften and the oil starts to separate from the mixture, about 5-7 minutes.
Add Dry Spices: Lower the heat and add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Sauté for about 30 seconds, stirring constantly, to avoid burning the spices. Add a tablespoon of water if the mixture seems too dry. Introduce Cauliflower: Add the cauliflower florets and salt to the pan. Mix well to coat the cauliflower with the spice mixture.

Cook Covered: Cover the pan and cook on low to medium heat for about 10-15 minutes, or until the cauliflower is tender-crisp. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking. If the mixture becomes too dry, you can sprinkle a little water. Add Garam Masala and Coriander: Once the cauliflower is cooked, stir in the garam masala and half of the chopped fresh coriander leaves.

Garnish and Serve: Transfer the Masala Gobhi to a serving dish. Garnish with the remaining fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with roti, paratha, rice, or as a side dish.

Enjoy your delicious Masala Gobhi
