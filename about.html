Excited to Know About Us..? Let's Go..


Welcome to CRAVING ROOTS! Namaste! I'm Rani Gopewar, the heart and hands behind Craving Roots. My journey with food began in my grandmother's bustling kitchen in Mumbai, filled with the intoxicating aromas of cardamom, turmeric, and simmering lentils. Those early experiences ignited a lifelong passion for the vibrant and diverse world of Indian vegetarian cuisine.

Here at Craving Roots, you'll discover a fresh take on classic Indian flavors. I believe that vegetarian cooking can be both exciting and deeply satisfying, and I'm here to show you how! You'll find recipes that:

Celebrate fresh, seasonal ingredients: I prioritize using locally sourced produce whenever possible to bring out the best flavors. Offer a modern twist on traditional dishes: While honoring the authentic roots of Indian cooking, I love to experiment with new techniques and flavor combinations to create innovative and approachable recipes.

Cater to busy lifestyles: Many of our recipes are designed for weeknight cooking, without compromising on taste or nutrition. Provide clear, step-by-step instructions: Whether you're a seasoned cook or just starting out, you'll find our recipes easy to follow, with helpful tips and variations along the way.

My mission with Craving Roots is to inspire you to explore the incredible possibilities of Indian vegetarian cooking and to bring the warmth and joy of these dishes into your own kitchen. I believe that food is not just sustenance, but a way to connect with culture, family, and ourselves.

Happy Cooking!
Warmly,
Rani Gopewar
Founder of Craving Roots
