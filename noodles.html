Simple Stir-Fried Noodles
Ingredients:
200g dried noodles (such as egg noodles, ramen noodles, or spaghetti)
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 inch ginger, grated
1/2 cup sliced vegetables (such as carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, or onions)
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional, for added flavor)
1 teaspoon sesame oil
Optional toppings: chopped green onions, sesame seeds, fried egg

Instructions:
Cook the Noodles: Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the dried noodles and cook according to the package instructions until just tender (al dente). Drain the noodles immediately and rinse them with cold water to stop the cooking process and prevent sticking. Set aside.

Saut√© Aromatics: Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant, being careful not to burn them.

Add Vegetables: Add the sliced vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly softened but still crisp.

Add Noodles and Sauce: Add the cooked noodles to the pan with the vegetables. Pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using). Toss everything together well to coat the noodles and vegetables evenly with the sauce.
Finish with Sesame Oil: Drizzle the sesame oil over the noodles and toss again.

Serve: Transfer the noodles to a serving dish. Garnish with chopped green onions, sesame seeds, or a fried egg, if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!

Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite vegetables and protein like chicken, shrimp, or tofu. Happy cooking!
